Welcome to Brandeis Students and Alumni Association

Brandeis University is a private research university with a liberal arts focus located in Waltham, Massachusetts. Our Students and Alumni Association aims to connect current students, recent graduates, and alumni through events, job opportunities, and more.

Alumni and affiliates of the university include former first lady of the United States Eleanor Roosevelt, Nobel Prize laureate Roderick MacKinnon and Fields Medalist Edward Witten, as well as foreign heads of state, congressmen, governors, diplomats, and recipients of the Nobel Prize, Pulitzer Prize, Academy Award, Emmy Award, and MacArthur Fellowship.
